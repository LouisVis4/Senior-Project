The prices that we pull are from Tiki's Geckos


Their prices are market average when it comes to online shopping for Geckos. Prices should be used as guidlines when purchasing crested geckos. Prices are only an average and could be found for cheaper at reptile expos or in physical locations.


A general guideline is that baby crested geckos go for the cheapest, then males, then females. This prices are often decided by breeders and for breeders making them the best source of knowing when to get a good deal on a crested gecko that you have been wanting.
